About me: Pursuing a Master's in Data Science at Uppsala University, Sweden. I have completed coursework in Statistical Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and Data Privacy and Security. I have worked on deep learning models, such as the transformer-based BERT classifier for sentiment analysis, and Differentially-private CNNs to discourage reverse-engineering attacks and prevent gradient leakage. Currently, I am implementing a Byzantine-robust Federated Learning framework with GANs to prevent malicious clients from compromising the learning process. Previously, I worked as a data analyst at Innovaccer, a US-based healthcare startup, building data ingestion and validation pipelines, and designing Power BI dashboards for visualizing and tracking KPIs.
